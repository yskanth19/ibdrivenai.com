Frequently asked questions

Is IBDriven free?

Yes, IBDriven is completely free to download and use. There is no subscription or in-app purchase.

Is my health data private?

Yes. We never sell your data. Your health profile is stored securely in your private Supabase account. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Email us at support@ibdrivenai.com with "Delete my account" in the subject line and we'll process it within 14 days.

Is IBDriven a medical app?

No. IBDriven provides general health information and AI-assisted guidance. It is not a medical device and does not provide diagnosis or treatment. Always consult your doctor.

Is Android coming?

Yes — Android support is in development. Sign up to be notified when it's available.

What AI powers IBDriven?

IBDriven's AI features are powered by Claude (Anthropic). Your conversations are processed with zero data retention.
